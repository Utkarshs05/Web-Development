About Us

SSIPMT, Raipur fosters an understanding of technical and management education to harvest skilled engineers and efficient managers for society.

Situated in the heart of Raipur, SSIPMT is among the 49 institutes in India selected for the AICTE IDEA Lab initiative. With NAAC and NBA accreditation, a 4-star IIC rating, and ARIIA ranking, SSIPMT ensures academic excellence.

Established in 2008, affiliated to CSVTU and approved by AICTE, the institute emphasizes industry-aligned learning, research, and all-round development of students.
