Welcome to SSIPMT

SSIPMT Raipur is one of the premier institutions in Central India imparting quality education in the field of Engineering and Management. The Institute is known for its modern infrastructure and a strong emphasis on holistic development.

SSIPMT, Raipur fosters technical and management education to develop skilled engineers and efficient managers. It is among the 49 institutes in India selected for the AICTE IDEA Lab, with high admissions, 64 patents, and 6+ startups.

SSIPMT offers UG, PG, and Doctoral programs in engineering, technology, and management fields such as B.Tech, M.Tech, MBA, and Ph.D.
